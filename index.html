About Us

The Washer Player's Club of America was founded in 2012 by Cody West.

It has grown the past 11 years into the compeditive club that it is today, stretching across our great nation.

Basic Rules of Play

Playing on regulation 3-Hole Boards - spaced 21ft apart - players can compete indivdually and in groups of two.

First team (or player) to reach 21 points wins the game. If a player goes over 21 points, they will bust back down do 15 points. Covers can happen when the other team throws a washer in the same hole - only.

Meeting Times & Events


The Washer Player's Club of America meets every third Saturday of the Month (@12pm local standard time), at three main locations across the U.S.

We also hold an Annual Tournament each year, bringing all club members to Arlington, TX - where everyone can compete for a Grand Prize of $1,000,000!
